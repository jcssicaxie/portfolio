Kitty Couture Fashion Magazine

Role
Editorial concept, layout, art direction
Format
Printed fashion magazine and mockup
Focus
Fast fashion awareness and long-lasting style

Kitty Couture is a creative magazine for young, curious fashion lovers that highlights the problems with fast fashion and promotes high-quality, long-lasting style.

It combines ideas from different sources to make learning about fashion fun and meaningful. Kitty Couture is about understanding how fashion connects to the world and inspiring thoughtful conversations.
